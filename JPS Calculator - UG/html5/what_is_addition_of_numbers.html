What is Addition of Numbers

Addition is a type of mathmatical calculation which is used to find the total quantity of something when they are couted together. + is the symbol for addition.

If you want to add two numbers, write the calculation as "1st number" + "2nd number" = "result".

Suppose you have 2 choclates in hand and you pick 3 more choclates from the chocolate jar. Now you want to know how many chocolates are in hand. Then, you want to add 2 and 3. So you should follow the below process:
  1. Write the numbers in a single line with + in between them; 2 + 3 = ?
  2. Count 3 numbers which comes after 2; 3, 4 and 5.

So, the result is "5".

How to Divide using JPS Calculater

Follow the below steps to devide two or more numbers using the JPS Calculator.

Make sure that you know what is division and its symbol.

  1. Open the JPS Calculator.
  2. Enter the numbers that you want to divide with the ÷ symbol between them.
  3. Press = to see the result.

You can see a single number on the JPS Calculator screen. This number is the result.

Enter 100 ÷ 2 ÷ 2 and press =. You can see 25 on the JPS Calculator screen.

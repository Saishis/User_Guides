What is Subtraction of Numbers

Subtraction is a type of mathmatical calculation which is used to find the quantity of something left in a lot, after some of it is removed from the lot. - is the symbol for addition.

If you want to subtract two numbers, write the calculation as "1st number" - "2nd number" = "result".

Note:
Usually, the 1st number is greater than the 2nd number in the process of subtraction.
Suppose you have 3 choclates in hand and you put 2 choclates back in the chocolate jar. Now you want to know how many chocolates you have in hand. Then, you want to subtract 2 from 3 (as you removed 2 out of 3 chocolates). So you should follow the below process:
  1. Write the numbers in a single line with - in between them; 3 - 2 = ?
  2. Count 2 numbers which comes before 3 backwards; 2 and 1.

So, the result is "1".
